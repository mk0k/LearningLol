Bomber

Bomber
Tix Cost 300
Level Required 0
Starting Cost $400
Damage Medium (3)
Damage Type Splash
Range Small (10)
Placement Ground

Bomber is a splash tower that specializes in crowd control. It throws tough bombs at incoming enemies, and can easily take care of hordes when in numbers.


Appearance

Bomber appears as a grey robloxian, wielding a classic roblox bomb.


Upgrades

Level 2 - Bigger Bombs

Cost: $300

  • Damage increased from 3 to 4. (+1)
  • Splash Radius increased from 4 to 6. (+2)
  • DPS increased from 1.2 to 1.6 (+0.4)
  • Bomber now has a bigger bomb.

Level 3 - Geared Up

Cost: $1125

  • Range increased from 10 to 12. (+2)
  • Damage increased from 4 to 6. (+2)
  • DPS increased from 1.6 to 3. (+1.4)
  • Bomber now wears goggles and gloves.

Level 4 - C4

Cost: $3200

  • Range increased from 12 to 14. (+2)
  • Damage increased from 6 to 12. (+6)
  • Splash Radius increased from 6 to 8. (+2)
  • Cooldown decreased from 2.5s to 2s. (-0.5s)
  • DPS increased from 3 to 6.86. (+3.86)
  • Bomber now has a C4.

Trivia

  • The Bomber was the first ever splash tower to be made in Retro Defense.
